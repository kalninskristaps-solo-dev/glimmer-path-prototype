## 1. Why this art (from the two parent docs)

| Insight | Art response |
|---------|----------------|
| CCS wins on **juice + safe mass theme** | Readable board, premium feedback, non-threatening world |
| CCS candy look is **owned mindshare + IP risk** | **No candy.** Light-motes / prism crystals instead |
| Better hooks need **endowment** | Grove map that visibly densifies and glows |
| Collections / seasons | Distinct seasonal palettes + codex illustration quality |
| Long-term product | Style guide that scales 3+ years without redrawing everything |
| Habit + calm mobile use | Cozy luminous, not aggressive arcade neon or grimdark |

**North star phrase:** *Luminous pastoral prism* — casual-clear, materially expensive, emotionally soft.

---

## 2. Style pillars

1. **Clarity first** — 6 board colors max; silhouette readable at thumb size.  
2. **Premium materials** — glass, glow, soft refraction; every tap feels valuable.  
3. **Ownership spectacle** — the grove is the second “main character” after the board.  
4. **Seasonal identity** — palette and flora shift; same engine, new dress.  
5. **Juice hierarchy** — small matches polite; rare combos *aurora-level* (variable reward look).  
6. **Original brand** — zero King candy language (no jelly beans, peppermint discs, “Sweet!” clones).

## 4. Shape language

- **Motes:** rounded “light seeds” — slightly faceted glass, not sharp gem grind.
- **Specials:** still instantly distinct (beam = elongated; burst = layered orb; prism core = multi-facet star).
- **Blockers:** *The Dim* — smoky glass, thorns of fog, cracked shells — readable threat without horror.
- **Characters:** soft stylization, large eyes, simple costume read; 1–2 primary guides (Luma + optional elder).
- **UI:** large radii, generous padding, one primary CTA color (luminous teal-gold).

**Avoid:** hyper-detailed board clutter; skeuomorphic candy plastic; flat generic “hypercasual” blobs with no material.

---

## 5. Materials & lighting

| Surface | Treatment |
|---------|-----------|
| Motes | Translucent + inner glow + soft specular |
| Board | Subtle frosted glass tray; soft shadow under pieces |
| Grove | Painterly midground, simpler far fog; parallax 2–3 layers |
| VFX | Additive light trails; particles as pollen / sparks, not confetti junk |
| Night mode (optional) | Board stays high contrast; world deeper indigo |

Lighting story: **path light is hope**. Dim regions physically darker on the map until cleared.

---

## 6. Juice & VFX tiers (addiction-relevant)

| Tier | When | Look / sound |
|------|------|----------------|
| 0 | Invalid swap | Soft settle, tiny click |
| 1 | Match 3 | Chime note + mote shatter-to-light |
| 2 | Cascade | Rising arpeggio, light trails link |
| 3 | Special create | Short brass-chime “forged” |
| 4 | Special combo | Screen bloom, aurora ribbon, bass thump |
| 5 | Level complete | Grove pulse on map + fanfare (short) |

**Variable reward rule:** Tier 4–5 must feel *rare enough* to stay special. Do not spam max juice every move.

**Fail juice:** gentle dim + soft thud; Luma sympathetic, not mocking. Shame art kills long-term love.

---

## 7. World map art (endowment vehicle)

- Top-down / slight isometric **grove path**.
- Unplayed regions: misted, desaturated, low fauna.
- Cleared regions: color, particles, sound beds unlock.
- Player “home glade” customizable: path stones, lanterns, frames (cosmetics).
- Episode gates: luminous arches, not candy locks.

**Performance:** modular props + palette swaps for seasons; do not unique-paint every node.

---

## 8. Character art

### Luma (guide)

- Small lantern-spirit; floating; warm core light.
- Expressions: joy, encourage, worry (streak risk — gentle).
- Appears in corners / pre-level; never blocks input.

### The Dim (force, not always personified)

- Seasonal avatars optional (Fog Warden, Storm Maw) — soft villain, not gore.
- Silhouette readable on event key art.

### Player avatar

- Optional stylized Pathkeeper silhouette for profile; not required on board.

---

## 9. UI / UX visual rules

- Touch targets ≥ 44pt equivalent.
- Pre-level: goal icons huge; boosters secondary.
- Win screen: stars + **grove delta** (“+1 firefly meadow”) — reinforce endowment.
- Shop: calm; no spinning fake wheels.
- Collection album: scrapbook glass pages; empty slots glow faintly (Zeigarnik).

**Typography**

- Titles: rounded geometric display (custom or licensed).
- Body: highly legible sans.
- No comic-candy display fonts that read as King-adjacent.

## 10. Audio direction (part of “art”)

| Layer | Direction |
|-------|-----------|
| Music | Soft ambient pastoral with subtle pulse; season variants |
| SFX | Glass, chimes, whoosh air — not cartoon “boing” candy kit |
| Voice | Minimal; Luma short VO optional, skippable |
| Mastering | Mobile-safe; music ducks under big juice |

Win jingle: **2 seconds max**, highly recognizable — earworm is a hook (legal).

---

## 11. Accessibility

- Colorblind patterns on all mote types.  
- High contrast UI mode.  
- Reduce motion: shorten cascades, cut screen shake.  
- Haptics optional per juice tier.  
- Text size scaling where platform allows.
